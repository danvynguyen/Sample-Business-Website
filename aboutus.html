About Us

From its humble beginnings operating from Rodrigo Falcone's Studford dorm room, Empura has grown to become a multinational comglomerate spanning across numerous countries, realms, nations, continents, and most importantly, people like you and me. Empura prides itself on its deep commitment to friends, family, and fun. Our success has been built on our corporate synergy, customer-centric incentive, our hyperlocal quotas, and our no-nonsense, by the rules, ecosystem that vales freemium touchpoints and "move the needle" big data and core competency. TLDR; "Our most valuable export is not our cars, our coffee, but delivering smiles." Not only do we think outside the box but our best practice is that unlike other corporations/yuppy trash, we reach out to our touch base and stakeholders. You can trust Empura to "put a pin in it."

The Secret to our Success

From being a leading developer in Artificial Intelligence and survellience to smaller scale projects such as the franchisation of Kreative Koffee which has opened nearly 150 shops across all realms, Empura has become a trusted household name known for its commitment to its clueless consumers, I meant customers. The secret of our success lies in our "by-the-numbers" approach based on our policy of "following the instructions." Our valuable workforce of like-minded employees all share our vision of the future ensuring that our company runs smoothly and crushes any semblence of individuality and dissent. Empura is also known as the first company to implement artificial intelligence into its workforce which is another aspect that gives Empura its advantage.

Rodrigo Falcone had a vision when he first founded Empura. A vision he wanted to share with the world. A bright future of everyone working side by side, creating a more stable and safe world through business and technology.
